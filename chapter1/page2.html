预格式化元素：PRE


PRE让浏览器将HTML文本中的内容按其本来的格式呈现。如下面的例子：




<PRE>
       Higher still and higher
         From the earth thou springest
       Like a cloud of fire;
         The blue deep thou wingest,
And singing still dost soar, and soaring ever singest.
</PRE>






在页面上的结果就是:




       Higher still and higher
         From the earth thou springest
       Like a cloud of fire;
         The blue deep thou wingest,
And singing still dost soar, and soaring ever singest.
